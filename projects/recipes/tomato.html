Tomato Pasta Recipe

Home

A comforting bowl of pasta coated in a rich, slow-simmered tomato sauce, bursting with the sweetness of ripe tomatoes and the fragrance of garlic and herbs. Fresh basil leaves bring a touch of brightness, while a sprinkle of Parmesan adds the perfect savory finish. Simple yet satisfying, this dish captures the essence of classic Italian home cooking.

Ingredients

  • 200g pasta (spaghetti, penne, or your choice)
  • 2 tbsp olive oil
  • 3 cloves garlic, finely chopped
  • 1 small onion, finely diced (optional)
  • 1 can (400g) chopped tomatoes or 4-5 fresh ripe tomatoes (blanched & chopped)
  • 1 tbsp tomato paste (optional, for richness)
  • 1 tsp sugar (to balance acidity)
  • 1 tsp dried oregano or italian herbs
  • Fresh basil leaves (a handful, torn)
  • Salt and black pepper to taste
  • Parmesan cheese (optional, for serving)

Instructions:

  1. Cook the pasta
    • Bring a pot of salted water to boil
    • Cook pasta until al dente (check package instructions)
    • Reserve about ½ cup of pasta water before draining
  2. Make the sauce
    • In a pan, heat olive oil over medium heat
    • Sauté garlic (and onion if using) until fragrant
    • Add tomatoes and tomato pasta. Cook for 8-10 minutes, stirring occasionally
    • Season with sugar, oregano, salt, and pepper. Simmer until slightly thickened
  3. Combine
    • Add drained pasta to the sauce. Toss well
    • If sauce feels too thick, stir in a splash of reserved pasta water
    • Mix in fresh basil leaves
  4. Serve
    • Top with grated Parmesan and extra basil if desired
    • Drizzle with a little olive oil for extra flavor

Variations:

  • Add chili flakes for a spicy kick
  • Stir in a splash of cream for a creamy tomato pasta
  • Add sautéed mushrooms, spinach, or grilled chicken for extra protein
